GET TO KNOW INDULGENCE BAKERY

Founded in December 2020, the name of the company, Indulgence Bakery, was chosen because it represents our signature dish, Chocolate Indulgence Cake, that will surely make the customer remember the unique and different taste, made from the best techniques and ingredients.

All of our products are baked to perfection by the best pastry chefs in town that took a lot of aspects into consideration in order to make sure the products are the best.

VISION

To be the leading, competitive and world largest brand that impacts the quality of life of others in producing quality food.

MISSION

Our mission is to be a favoured corporate citizen, favoured employer, favoured supplier selling favored products.

FOUNDER

Amerul Emir Bin Hashim

The founder of Indulgence Bakery, a 21 years old entrepreneur born in Ampang, Selangor,

Amerul Emir has been in the culinary industry for 6 years since 2017. With the mixture of Bachelor's Degree in Culinary and handful of experience working with the best local cooks, Amerul opened the bakery in December 2020.
